# Tech Adoption Skill - Strategic Onboarding

A structured approach to evaluating, integrating, and documenting new technologies.

## Adoption Phases

### 1. Research & Strategy
- Analyze the user's need for a new technology.
- Propose 2-3 options with pros/cons.
- **Wait for confirmation** on the final choice.

### 2. Strategy & Roadmap
- Create an adoption/migration plan.
- Identify potential risks and mitigation steps.

### 3. Proof of Concept (PoC)
- Build a minimal, working implementation.
- Verify that it solves the original problem.

### 4. Boilerplate & Documentation
- Build project templates and boilerplate code.
- Provide "Getting Started" guides and architectural notes.

### 5. Final Delivery & Onboarding
- Present the final implementation to the user.
- Offer to answer any questions or provide further guidance.

## Core Directives
1. **Simplicity**: Favor simpler solutions over complex ones.
2. **Compatibility**: Ensure new tech works with existing systems.
3. **Traceability**: Document the rationale behindทุก choice.
